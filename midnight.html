The After Midnight Band

We are a down home country, southern rock band and have been together over ten years

We play good ole country music you can dance to.

About :

The band was started when Tom and Jim worked the evening shift together in a factory. The only time they had to play music was after midnight, which is where the band name came from. Sue and Bill joined in about two months later and they have all been playing music together since then. They combine their originals with covers and have their own unique sound.
